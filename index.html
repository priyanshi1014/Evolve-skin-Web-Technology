FACE WASH

Facewash used cleanse the face by removing dirt, oil, makeup, and impurities from the skin. It typically comes in various formulations, such as gel, foam, cream, or exfoliating scrub, to suit different skin types—oily, dry, sensitive, or combination. A good facewash can leave your skin feeling refreshed, clean, and balanced without stripping away its natural moisture. Regular use can help prevent acne, reduce excess oil, and improve overall skin texture.

FACE CREAM

Face cream is a moisturizing product designed to hydrate, nourish, and protect the skin. It often contains ingredients like hyaluronic acid, vitamins, and antioxidants that help maintain skin's moisture balance, improve elasticity, and combat signs of aging. Face creams are available for different skin types—dry, oily, combination, or sensitive—and may offer additional benefits like sun protection (with SPF), brightening, or anti-aging effects. Using a face cream regularly can leave your skin feeling soft, smooth, and supple, helping to create a healthy and radiant complexion. Choosing the right face cream for your skin type is crucial for optimal results.

SUNSCREEN

Sunscreen is a skincare essential that protects the skin from harmful UV rays, preventing sunburn, premature aging, and reducing the risk of skin cancer. It comes in various forms, including lotions, creams, gels, and sprays, and is often labeled with a Sun Protection Factor (SPF) rating that indicates its effectiveness. Sunscreens are available in different formulations for various skin types and needs, such as water-resistant options for swimming or sweat-resistant formulas for outdoor activities.
